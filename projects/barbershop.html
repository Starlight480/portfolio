Who We Are

More Than a Barbershop

Sharp Cut Barbers started in 2021 when founder Emeka Nwankwo noticed every barbershop in Ajah was the same — long waits, rushed cuts, and zero attention to detail. He wanted to change that.

We built a space where guys come to hang out as much as they come to get fresh. Free Wi-Fi, cold drinks, and barbers who actually listen. Every cut comes with a guarantee — if it's not right, we fix it. Free.

Walk in as a customer. Leave as family. ✊
