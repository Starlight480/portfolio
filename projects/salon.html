Our Story

Beauty with Soul

Velvet Beauty Studio was born from a simple belief: every woman deserves a space where she feels pampered, heard, and beautiful. Founded by Adaeze Obi in 2022, we started as a small studio in her living room.

Today, we're a team of 8 talented stylists and estheticians serving women across Lagos. We've styled brides, influencers, and everyday queens who just want to treat themselves.

Come for the service, stay for the vibes. 💕
