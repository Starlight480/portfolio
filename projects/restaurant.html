Our Story

Where Lagos Meets the Grill

Founded in 2023, The Orange Grill started as a roadside suya spot that quickly became Lekki's worst-kept secret. Our founder, Chidi Okonkwo, combined his grandmother's spice recipes with a passion for open-flame cooking.

Today, we're a full-service restaurant with over 40 dishes on our menu, but we've never lost touch with what made us special — bold flavours, honest portions, and a space where everyone feels at home.

Whether you're here for a quick lunch or a weekend dinner with friends, we've got you covered.
